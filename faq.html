How can we help you?

Find answers to all your questions about BrainBloom programs

📚 Programs

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer programs for children aged 4-16 years, with age-appropriate curriculum designed for each developmental stage. From early learners to teenagers, we have something for everyone.

What is the class size?

We maintain small class sizes of 8-12 students to ensure personalized attention. Our student-to-teacher ratio is 6:1, allowing each child to receive the guidance they need.

What qualifications do your teachers have?

All our mentors are certified educators with minimum 5 years of experience in their respective fields. They undergo rigorous training in our unique teaching methodology.

What is the duration of each program?

Programs run for 8-12 weeks per term, with classes held once or twice weekly. Each session is 60-90 minutes depending on the age group and program type.

📝 Enrollment

How do I enroll my child?

Enrollment is easy! Simply click the SignUp button, fill out the registration form, choose your preferred programs, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Are there any trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for first-time students. You can experience our teaching methodology and see if it's the right fit before committing to a full program.

When can I enroll?

Enrollment is open year-round! However, spots fill up quickly. We recommend enrolling at least 2 weeks before your desired start date to secure your spot.

Is there a waiting list?

Yes, for popular time slots we maintain a waiting list. You'll be notified as soon as a spot becomes available, typically within 1-2 weeks.

💰 Fees & Payments

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by program, starting from $199/month. Discounts available for siblings and annual payments. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee within the first 7 days of enrollment if you're not satisfied. For cancellations after that, we provide pro-rated refunds.

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs! All learning materials, including workbooks, art supplies, and digital resources, are provided as part of the program fee.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we have a limited number of merit-based scholarships. Contact our admissions team for application details and deadlines.

📅 Schedules

What are the class timings?

Classes run Monday-Friday from 3 PM - 7 PM, and weekends from 9 AM - 2 PM. Multiple time slots available for each program.

Can I change my schedule?

Yes, schedule changes are allowed with 48 hours notice, subject to availability. Contact our support team for assistance.

Do you have holiday programs?

Yes! We offer special holiday camps during school breaks - spring break, summer, and winter holidays. Check our events page for upcoming dates.

What if we miss a class?

Make-up classes are available within the same term, subject to availability. Simply book a make-up session through your parent portal.
