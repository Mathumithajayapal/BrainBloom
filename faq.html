How can we help you?

Find answers to all your questions about BrainBloom programs

📚 Programs

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer programs for children aged 4-16 years, with age-appropriate curriculum designed for each developmental stage.

What is the class size?

We maintain small class sizes of 8-12 students to ensure personalized attention.

What qualifications do your teachers have?

All our mentors are certified educators with minimum 5 years of experience.

What is the duration of each program?

Programs run for 8-12 weeks per term, with classes held once or twice weekly.

📝 Enrollment

How do I enroll my child?

Simply click the SignUp button, fill out the registration form, choose your preferred programs, and complete the payment.

Are there any trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for first-time students.

When can I enroll?

Enrollment is open year-round! We recommend enrolling at least 2 weeks before your desired start date.

Is there a waiting list?

Yes, for popular time slots we maintain a waiting list.

💰 Fees & Payments

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by program, starting from $199/month. Discounts available for siblings and annual payments.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee within the first 7 days of enrollment.

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs! All learning materials are provided as part of the program fee.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we have a limited number of merit-based scholarships.

📅 Schedules

What are the class timings?

Classes run Monday-Friday from 3 PM - 7 PM, and weekends from 9 AM - 2 PM.

Can I change my schedule?

Yes, schedule changes are allowed with 48 hours notice, subject to availability.

Do you have holiday programs?

Yes! We offer special holiday camps during school breaks.

What if we miss a class?

Make-up classes are available within the same term, subject to availability.
